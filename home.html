What is this site?

This site is intended to supplement the content of the 'CSS Essentials' section to CSC1026. It covers the lecture note material and adds additional background content for understanding and further use.

How to use the site?

Select a section from the menu on the left to load the related content. Use the arrows at the bottom to step through each piece of information.

The internal links navigate through the tutorial site's content, and the external links lead to relevant pages on Computer Science and Newcastle University.

The last option is a review quiz that can be taken and marked after viewing the whole site to test your knowledge. Alternatively, you could take the quiz before viewing the content to see what needs to be revised.
